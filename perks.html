The Archery Perks Overhaul focuses on three diverging perk paths: general, speed, and power. You can learn as many general perks as you wish but will have to choose between either speed or power as your specialization. This means that once you learn a perk from the speed path, you cannot learn any perks from the power path and vice versa.

General Perks

These perks are helpful for all archery play styles, and are the core set of perks.


Speed Perks

These perks reward you for fighting at close range with a bow and allow you to better defend yourself against nearby enemies.


Power Perks

These perks reward you for keeping your distance from enemies and taking time to line up a perfect shot.


Skill Tree

The archery perk tree was rebuilt to complement the diverging perk paths.


General perks

Hunter's Discipline
(Lvl: 0)

Recover twice as many arrows from dead bodies.

Eagle Eye
(Lvl: 30, 60)

Pressing Block while aiming will (moderately, greatly) zoom in your view.

Hunter's Reflex
(Lvl: 30, 60)

Pressing block while aiming will slow time by (30%, 60%).

Makeshift Arrows
(Lvl: 40)

Unlock silent and blink arrow crafting recipes at the forge.

Opportunist
(Lvl: 50, 80)

Shooting an enemy in the back deals (35%, 70%) more damage.

Missilecraft
(Lvl: 90)

Unlock barbed and crippling arrow crafting recipes at the forge.

Speed perks

Speed Training
(Lvl: 20, 50)

Specialize in fast attacks and close ranged combat with bows. Bows fire (25%, 50%) faster.

Point Blank
(Lvl: 30, 60)

Bows deal (even) more damage the closer you are to your target.

40-30 feet: Deal (10%, 15%) more damage.
30-20 feet: Deal (20%, 30%) more damage.
20-10 feet: Deal (30%, 45%) more damage.
10-00 feet: Deal (40%, 60%) more damage.

Jolt
(Lvl: 40, 80)

Arrows stagger all but the largest opponents (25%, 50%) of the time.

Ranger
(Lvl: 50)

Drawing a bow no longer slows you down.

Knockout
(Lvl: 70)

Bashing with a bow knocks the target to the ground. 15 second cooldown.

Rapid Fire
(Lvl: 100)

Shots that hit the same target in quick succession deal 10% more damage per hit, up to 50%.

Power perks

Power Training
(Lvl: 20, 50)

Specialize in strong attacks and long ranged combat with bows. Bows deal (25%, 50%) more damage.

Sharpshooter
(Lvl: 30, 60)

Bows deal (even) more damage the further you are from your target.

40-80 feet: Deal (10%, 15%) more damage.
80-150 feet: Deal (25%, 35%) more damage.
80-150 feet: Deal (40%, 60%) more damage.
300+ feet: Deal (60%, 100%) more damage.

Critical Shot
(Lvl: 40, 80)

(15%, 20%) chance of a critical hit that does extra damage (50% more critical damage).

Power Stance
(Lvl: 50)

Deal 30% more damage while you are stationary.

Stalker
(Lvl: 70)

Bows have a 100% chance to critically hit when fired from out of combat.

Overdraw
(Lvl: 100)

Overdraw a bow for up to 5 seconds to deal up to 100% more damage.
