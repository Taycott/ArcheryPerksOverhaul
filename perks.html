The Archery Perks Overhaul focuses on three diverging perk paths: general, speed, and power. You will specialize in either speed or power. This means that once you learn a perk from the speed path, you cannot learn any perks from the power path and vice versa.

General Perks

These perks are helpful for all archery play styles, and are the core set of perks.


Speed Perks

These perks reward you for fighting at close range with a bow and allow you to better defend yourself against nearby enemies.


Power Perks

These perks reward you for keeping your distance from enemies and taking time to line up a perfect kill shot.


Skill Tree

The archery perk tree was rebuilt to complement the diverging perk paths.


General perks

Makeshift Arrows
(Lvl: 0)

Unlock silent, noisemaker, and luminous arrow crafting recipes at the forge.

Eagle Eye
(Lvl: 30, 60)

Pressing Block while aiming will zoom in your view (further).

Hunter's Reflex
(Lvl: 30, 60)

Pressing block while aiming will slow time by (30%, 60%).

Archer's Toolbox
(Lvl: 40)

Unlock blink, tracker, and grappling arrow crafting recipes at the forge.

Opportunist
(Lvl: 50, 80)

Shooting an enemy in the back deals (50%, 100%) more damage.

Missilecraft
(Lvl: 90)

Unlock barbed, explosive, and sleeping arrow crafting recipes at the forge.

Speed perks

Speed Training
(Lvl: 20, 50)

Specialize in fast attacks and close ranged combat with bows. Bows fire (25%, 50%) faster.

Point Blank
(Lvl: 30, 60)

Bows deal (even) more damage the closer you are to your target.

40-30 feet: Deal (10%, 15%) more damage.
30-20 feet: Deal (20%, 30%) more damage.
20-10 feet: Deal (30%, 45%) more damage.
10-00 feet: Deal (40%, 60%) more damage.

Jolt
(Lvl: 40, 70)

Arrows stagger all but the largest opponents (20%, 40%) of the time.

Ranger
(Lvl: 50)

Able to move faster with a drawn bow and recover twice as many arrows from dead bodies.

Knockout
(Lvl: 60, 90)

Bashing with a bow knocks the target to the ground (and prevents magicka and stamina usage for 8 seconds). This ability can be used once every 15 seconds.

Rapid Fire
(Lvl: 100)

Shots that hit the same target in quick succession deal 10% more damage per hit, up to 50%.

Power perks

Power Training
(Lvl: 20, 50)

Specialize in strong attacks and long ranged combat with bows. Bows deal (25%, 50%) more damage.

Sharpshooter
(Lvl: 30, 60)

Bows deal (even) more damage the further you are from your target.

40-80 feet: Deal (10%, 15%) more damage.
80-150 feet: Deal (25%, 35%) more damage.
80-150 feet: Deal (25%, 35%) more damage.
300+ feet: Deal (60%, 100%) more damage.

Critical Shot
(Lvl: 40, 70)

(15%, 20%) chance of a critical hit that does extra damage (50% more critical damage).

Power Stance
(Lvl: 50, 80)

Deal (15%, 30%) more damage while you are stationary.

Stalker
(Lvl: 60)

Bows have a 100% chance to critically hit when fired from out of combat.

Overdraw
(Lvl: 100)

Overdraw a bow for up to 5 seconds to deal up to 100% more damage.
