The Archery Perks Overhaul adds unique, craftable arrows to the game. These arrows can be crafted after learning the corresponding perk.

Rank 1 - Makeshift Arrows

Silent Arrow

This arrow is silent.

10 arrow crafting recipe:
10 Ebony Arrows
3 Hagraven Feathers
1 Hagraven Claw

Blink Arrow

This arrow travels at an extremely fast speed.

10 arrow crafting recipe:
10 Elven Arrows
1 Orange Dartwing

Rank 2 - Missilecraft

Barbed Arrow

This arrow deals additional bleeding damage and pierces armor.

10 arrow crafting recipe:
10 Daedric Arrows
1 Fire Salts
1 Frost Salts

Crippling Arrow

This arrow slows its target's movement and attack speed for 5 seconds.

10 arrow crafting recipe:
10 Ancient Nordic Arrows
1 Ice Wraith Teeth
