The Archery Perks Overhaul adds unique, craftable arrows to the game. These arrows can be crafted after learning certain perks, and require the following ingredients: Firewood, corresponding material ingot, mysterious ore. Mysterious ore is a new item that can be found on dead bodies if you killed them with a bow. The new arrows are separated into three ranks based on how powerful they are, and each rank is unlocked by a different perk.

Rank 1

Silent Arrow

This arrow is silent.

24 arrow crafting recipe:
1 Firewood
1 ebony ingot
8 mysterious ore

Noisemaker Arrow

This arrow makes a loud noise that attracts nearby enemies.

24 arrow crafting recipe:

1 dwarven ingot
